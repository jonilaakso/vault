# What is APT?
APT (Advanced Persistent Threat) refers to a prolonged and targeted cyberattack where an intruder gains unauthorized access to a network and remains undetected for an extended period. APTs are often carried out by highly skilled threat actors, typically for espionage, data theft, or sabotage.


**Common Attack techniques:**

* Social Engineering
* Zero-day attacks
* Supply chain attacks
* Rootkits
* Command and control servers
